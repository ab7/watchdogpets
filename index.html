Home

Welcome to Watchdog, a pet sitting service that has provided in home, customized pet care since 1983.

Your pets are part of your family, so a wonderful way to give everyone peace of mind when you are gone is to know they are safe and happy in their own home. Plus, our pet sitters will keep an eye on your house!

Services We Offer:

  • Fresh food and water on a regular schedule
  • Walk dogs, if desired
  • Scoop litter boxes
  • Play with your pet(s)
  • Give medications
  • Pull in mail and newspapers
  • Water plants
  • Rotate lights and adjust curtains

All of this is included in one flat fee per visit. We offer one or two visits per day, and a third visit can be arranged depending upon availability. Watchdog has experience in taking care of: horses, rabbits, ferrets, fish, birds, guinea pigs and turtles, as well as dogs and cats.
